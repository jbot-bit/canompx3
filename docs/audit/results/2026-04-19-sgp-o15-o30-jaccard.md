### Lane Definition
**O15 lane:**
```sql
SELECT DISTINCT trading_day
FROM orb_outcomes o
JOIN daily_features d 
  ON (o.trading_day, o.symbol, o.orb_minutes) = (d.trading_day, d.symbol, d.orb_minutes)
WHERE symbol='MNQ' AND orb_label='SINGAPORE_OPEN' AND entry_model='E2'
  AND confirm_bars=1 AND rr_target=1.5 AND orb_minutes=15
```

## Verdict: WARN

**Jaccard = 0.6493** falls in the WARN band (0.50–0.70).

The two lanes are **statistically distinct** — they represent separate apertures of the same filter on the same session, and fire on non-identical day sets (~80% overlap). However, the **65% overlap is material enough to merit capacity review** before deploying both at full size.

### Interpretation

1. **Distinct underlying structure:** O15 and O30 apertures respond to intraday ORB formation differently. Of 596 total eligible days, 110 days fire only at O15 and 99 days fire only at O30. This 11–12% exclusive fire rate per lane indicates real aperture differentiation.

2. **Moderate redundancy risk:** 387 of 596 days (65%) see both apertures fire simultaneously. If capital is split equally between O15 and O30, ~65% of risk is correlated (same-day same-direction entry on both). This creates portfolio drag unless:
   - O15 and O30 have materially different expected returns (offset via position sizing), OR
   - The exclusive-fire days (35% of calendar) provide sufficient decorrelation benefit

3. **Expected return check (Mode B grandfathered numbers):**
   - O15: ExpR = +0.1087 R/trade
   - O30: ExpR = +0.1251 R/trade (Δ = +0.0164, ~15% higher per trade)
   
   The **15% ExpR spread is modest but real.** If ExpR translates to annualized R (assuming ~47 fires/year per Mode A sample size), O30's ~1.5% extra annual R may justify the redundancy. Mode A revalidation (trading_day < 2026-01-01 only) needed to confirm.

4. **Capacity implication:** If deploying both lanes:
   - **Option A (full parallel):** Deploy both at N=1 contracts each. Portfolio-level redundancy ≈ 65% (drag). But O30's higher ExpR offers total-R upside on exclusive days.
   - **Option B (selective, reduce overlap):** Route only O30 (higher ExpR). Trade O15 only on exclusive-fire days detected at session-start. Requires real-time eligibility tracking.
   - **Option C (weighted allocation):** Allocate 40% capital to O15 + 60% to O30 (reflects ExpR ratio). Maintains both lanes; redundancy tax is managed via sizing.
