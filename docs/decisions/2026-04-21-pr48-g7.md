## Methodology verification (prerequisite to running)

**Hypothesis (from pre-reg H1_MNQ / H1_MES / H1_MGC lines 91-104):**
> "pooled OLS pnl_r ~ rank_within_lane(rel_vol) + lane_FE, HC3 SE, one-tailed β₁ > 0, t ≥ +3.0, p < 0.05."

Three independent per-instrument K=1 replications (pre-reg § testing_discipline.K_framing lines 108-115).

**Appropriate nulls for this hypothesis (verified before running):**

1. **Destruction shuffle — MATCHES.** Shuffle `pnl_r` within each lane; recompute β₁. Destroys the rel_vol → pnl_r link while preserving marginal distributions and lane structure. Under H0, shuffled β₁ is centered at zero with SE matching observed SE.

2. **RNG null — MATCHES.** Replace `rank_within_lane(rel_vol)` with uniform[0,1] random ranks; recompute β₁ against real pnl_r. Tests whether `rel_vol` carries specific information vs a generic covariate. Under H0, β₁ ≈ 0.

Both nulls directly attack the H1 claim. No label-shuffle on instrument assignment is required because the hypothesis is per-instrument, not cross-instrument structural. Methodology is aligned with H1 as written. No METHODOLOGY_MISMATCH; proceeded to Step B.
