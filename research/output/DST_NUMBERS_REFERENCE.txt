================================================================================
KEY TAKEAWAY
================================================================================

The "contradiction" between positive expectancy (+0.8368) and negative actual
returns (-0.4879) is NOT a bug but a CATEGORY DIFFERENCE:

  Expectancy_r:
    • Calculated: from yearly_results (wins only per year)
    • Meaning: R per winning period if you ignore losses
    • Use for: validation signal, NOT portfolio impact
    • Result: POSITIVE
    
  Avg_r (actual outcomes):
    • Calculated: across ALL entry signals ever taken
    • Meaning: True portfolio impact per trade
    • Use for: expected value per trade taken
    • Result: NEGATIVE

The dst_winter_avg_r (-0.3491) and dst_summer_avg_r (-0.5568) show the REAL
market performance degradation due to DST misalignment.

This is not a statistical error. It's a reflection that fixed sessions drift
with DST while market events stay fixed.
